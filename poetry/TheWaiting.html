Poetry › The Waiting
I sit, waiting on the clock,
has it changed? I know not
but what's beyond
the silver lining of the clock

I can feel the time breeze,
but still, it's not even
quarter the way to
the silver lining of the clock

Now it's close
yet still I sit,
because the passing
has still not hit
the silver lining of the clock

It has hit, I am free,
I breeze by, not stopping
And now it's clear
the Black lining of the clock

Written date: January 1st, 2015
