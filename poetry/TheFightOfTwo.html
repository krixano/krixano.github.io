Poetry › The Fight of Two
The constant storm,
I dread of it
First it's still,
And then it's hit

I happy thee,
Friendly among them
But lies I find,
So, have I, sprung condemn

Oh! It lies, it lies!
It knows not what of me
The deceit it brings,
Transforms all to she!

I understand not,
of all these deceits
But understand this,
with all, shall come defeats!

Written date: Feb. 14th, 2015
