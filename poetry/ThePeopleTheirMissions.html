Poetry › The People, Their Missions
grieved heart,
a wickedness, heart thoughts
No favor found
I blot it all out
It seeps through

it's poisoning,
Can I hide?
I need to hide
sleep in a darkness
blocked on the outside
bricks to hold it back
I don't want to feel anymore,
I feel too much, too often

the disappointment,
Can I forget?
I need to forget
the people their missions
their clueless sickness
that spreads the world
Can I escape it this time?
