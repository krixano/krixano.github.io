Poetry › Go or Grow
Death,
How does it feel?
painful breath,
And movement still?

Or is it relief
of life lived,
or maybe grief
of life not gived?

Is it cold
as can be,
or is there warmth
like relaxing tea?

But of all these,
There's one I must know,
what's beyond this death of me?
Do I go, or do I grow?

Written date: July 1st, 2015
