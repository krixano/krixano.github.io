Poetry › What Right Turn?
Tell me how
to make this right
to stop the doubt
to keep on driving

The weight of how I was
following through me
winding through every
cracked road of

Wrong turns
crashing
into the boulders
not seen

The reminders, the doubt
Shutting me down
keeping myself
away from everyone else

Tell me how
to replace this hole
to replace this anger
this hate
of myself
to know how,
to want,
to keep on driving

Written date: June 5, 2020
