Poetry › Hate
I hate myself
for being terrible
I'm terrible
for hating myself

My sickness spreads
and comes back
amplifying
my sickness

I think of
saving others,
the world,
from myself
But hurting
the closest ones -
I'm stuck

I save to hurt, or
I hurt to save

I feel hopeless
Unknown
Everything

I don't want
to be known
to feel
to hurt

I can't find
meaning
reflections
myself
purpose

What if my purpose
is to have no purpose?
or, no self-purpose?

Written date: June 24, 2020
