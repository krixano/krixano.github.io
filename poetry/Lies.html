Poetry › Lies
Your lies don't erase the truth,
Rather, the truth always remains
And when it comes out, as it always does
You'll look like a fool, who claims

Instead, try
try to be truly happy
your deceptions only give you a fool's happiness
It isn't true, it isn't full or long
it isn't the best kind, that can prolong

Your own happiness is yours,
it is your own fault
it is your own curse,
or it is your gift
as well as your nurse

Well, whatever you choose,
it is your right,
But,
It's also your fight
