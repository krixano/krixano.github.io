Poetry › Superficial
the peels
each a jabbing, scratching, lifting
stable, solid, remaining
yet smaller left with each

As protection wards off
and a lost color, between -
a response in cursing -
- each making tears artificial
- regardless of the benefits
and turning back in disregard

It does live,
Feeling, yet shown not
it itself masks
not truly, but
must - must
it's selfish -
selfless

Don't they know
don't they see
the absorbing, in
origin waters
it lasts
remains
truly remains
truly superficial

What's shown's not seen
what's daring not to be,
Seen

Written date: Feb. 13th, 2018
