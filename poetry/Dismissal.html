Poetry › Dismissal
You dismiss us, why?
		Why shall I say you?
So I know to try
		I will not be cruel!
You already are
		I am not, am with thee
But now you're afar!
		Just leave me be!

Alas, She has left!
Sadness unto me
Has it crept
Unto thee?
Oh, just leave us be!

Written date: Feb. 14th, 2015
