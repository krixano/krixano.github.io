Poetry › The Crook
Tight close, Heat froze,
No breathing through my nose,
No movement in my clothes
The haunting of eyes
penetrating my skin
Do they see my lies?
I know they see within!

The disaproval look
The thoughts,
they may have took.
But they are who rot
As they see my faithful crook!

Written date: Feb. 14th, 2015
